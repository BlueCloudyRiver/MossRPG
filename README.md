# Moss

**Moss** is an RPG built with Rust.

## Story

Moss is a distant planet located millions of light-years from Earth. It is home to two intelligent species: the **Kaelor** and the **Aven**.

Neither species is inherently good or evil. For generations, they have shared the same world, but differences in culture, beliefs, and competing claims over land and resources have fueled a long-standing rivalry. While periods of peace have existed, the fragile balance between the two civilizations has repeatedly collapsed into conflict.

You play as a young Kaelor who crosses into Aven territory for a mission whose true significance unfolds over time. As you journey deeper into unfamiliar lands, you face hostile creatures, Aven warriors, ancient dungeons, and powerful bosses. Each region presents greater challenges than the last, testing your combat skills while revealing the history behind the conflict.

As the story progresses, you begin to question whether the rivalry between the Kaelor and the Aven is truly inevitable—or whether there is a deeper force shaping the fate of Moss itself.
